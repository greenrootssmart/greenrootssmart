Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

Can I start free and upgrade later? +
Yes! Start with our free Starter plan anytime. Upgrade to Basic, Pro or Premium whenever your business grows. No pressure!
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all Indian debit & credit cards, net banking, and EMI options via Razorpay.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! Cancel anytime from your dashboard with zero cancellation fee. Your plan continues until the end of the billing period.
Is GST invoice provided? +
Yes! GST invoice is automatically generated for every payment. You can download it from your dashboard anytime for accounting purposes.
What is the difference between Basic and Pro? +
Pro gives you Featured listing in search (huge visibility boost!), Verified badge, unlimited enquiries, WhatsApp alerts, and lead export. Most serious sellers choose Pro!
Do you offer discounts for annual plans? +
Yes! Pay annually and save 20% on all paid plans. Switch to annual billing from the toggle above to see discounted prices.
