From Ooty to the World —
A Green Dream Begins

Green Roots Smart was born in the hills of Ooty, Tamil Nadu — a place surrounded by nature's beauty. Our founder Dinesh Chennamallan saw a simple but powerful problem: thousands of eco-friendly businesses across India had amazing green products, but no easy way to reach buyers who cared about sustainability.

The existing B2B platforms were cluttered with millions of products — making it impossible for genuine eco suppliers to stand out. Buyers who wanted to source green products had no trusted, dedicated platform to find verified eco suppliers.

So we built one. Green Roots Smart — India's first dedicated B2B marketplace exclusively for sustainable and eco-friendly businesses.

"Every supplier on our platform is making the world a little greener. Our job is to help them reach more buyers — in India and across the world."

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From an idea in Ooty to India's green B2B revolution

💡
Early 2024
The Idea is Born
Dinesh Chennamallan identifies the gap — India has thousands of eco businesses but no dedicated B2B platform. Green Roots Smart concept is born in Ooty.
🎨
Mid 2024
Research & Design
Market research across 500+ eco businesses in Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra and Gujarat. Platform design and branding completed. Domains registered.
⚙️
Late 2024
Building the Platform
Development begins. Website built with Next.js, Supabase database, Razorpay payments. First 50 suppliers onboarded for beta testing.
🚀
2025
Official Launch
Green Roots Smart officially launches. 500+ suppliers join in first month. Featured in YourStory and Inc42 as India's most promising green startup.
🌍
2026 & Beyond
Going Global
Expanding to South Asia, Middle East and Europe. Mobile app launch. 12,400+ suppliers. 50+ countries. The green revolution is just beginning!
