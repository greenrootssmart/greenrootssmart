The Team

Meet Our Founder

The person behind India's first eco B2B marketplace.

D
Dinesh Chennamallan
Founder & CEO — Green Roots Smart

Born and raised in the beautiful Nilgiris, Ooty, Tamil Nadu — Dinesh has always been passionate about nature and sustainable living. He founded Green Roots Smart with a clear vision: to create India's first dedicated B2B marketplace exclusively for eco-friendly businesses. With zero compromise on sustainability, he is building a platform that connects verified green suppliers with buyers across India and the world.

"Every supplier on our platform is making the world a little greener. Our job is to help them reach more buyers — in India and across the world. Green business is the future of India."
