🏢 About SunGrow Solar
SunGrow Solar Pvt Ltd is one of India's leading solar energy companies, founded in 2016 in Pune, Maharashtra. We specialize in manufacturing and supplying high-efficiency monocrystalline solar panels, solar inverters, and complete EPC solutions.

With over 8 years of experience and 450+ successful installations across 18 Indian states, we are trusted for quality, reliability and after-sales service. Our panels come with 25-year performance warranty.

We export to UAE, Singapore, Kenya, Bangladesh and 8 other countries. Products are BIS certified and meet IEC international standards.
