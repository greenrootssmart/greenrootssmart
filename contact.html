Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Green Roots Smart

How do I list my business on Green Roots Smart? +
Click "List Your Business" button, create a free account, fill your business profile and start adding products. It takes less than 10 minutes to get started!
Is it free to register as a buyer? +
Yes! Buyer registration is 100% free. Browse products, send enquiries and connect with verified eco suppliers across India at absolutely no cost.
How are suppliers verified on the platform? +
Our team manually verifies every supplier through document checks, GST verification, business proof and eco certification review before listing them on the platform.
Can I sell internationally from India? +
Yes! Pro and Premium sellers get access to international buyers from 50+ countries. We help with export documentation and global trade support.
How do I send an enquiry to a supplier? +
Browse any product or supplier and click the "Enquire" button or directly message via WhatsApp. You can also send bulk RFQ requests from your buyer dashboard.
What types of eco products are listed on GRS? +
We list products across 10+ categories including Solar Energy, Organic Farming, Eco Packaging, Water Technology, Wind Energy, Green Building, Herbal Products, Energy Storage and more.
How do I claim my business profile? +
If your business is already listed on GRS, visit greenrootssmart.com/claim-profile, search your business name, fill the verification form and our team will approve within 24-48 hours.
How do I report a fake or non-eco supplier? +
Email us at [email protected] or WhatsApp us at +91 63554 08878. We investigate within 24 hours and take strict action against non-compliant suppliers.
